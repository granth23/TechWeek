Workshops

The fest provides a range of workshops on cutting-edge technologies, led by experts, to empower participants with hands-on learning opportunities. Attendees can expand their technical knowledge, network with like-minded individuals, and stay up-to-date on the latest technological advancements. It is an unmissable opportunity for anyone seeking to deepen their understanding of cutting-edge technology.
