Participation Guidelines

Topic: Futuristic technology
No. of Teams per school: 1
No. of Participants per team: 3
Eligibility: Classes IX-XII
Finals Date: 10/09/2020
Time: 6.5 Minutes

General Guidelines

Participants are required to create a website and a video trailer based on it. Selected teams will present on 10th followed by questioning by the judges. Judging will be based originality, creativity, design, technical skills and interpretation.

Website Guidelines

• Use of templates is not allowed
• Video should be embedded in the website.
• Voiceover (if any) should not be computerised.

Video Guidelines

• Can use any software to create the movie.
• Clips from external sources can be used.
• Subtitles to be included.
• The trailer must be of 2-3 mins.
• Video to be uploaded in .mp4 format
